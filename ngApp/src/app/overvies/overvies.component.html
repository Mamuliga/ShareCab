About ShareCab

ShareCab is friendly traveling service with carpooling. It is smooth and smart travel, with the emphasis on safety for all. ShareCab is a trusted community marketplace that connects drivers with empty seats to co-travelers looking for a ride. ShareCab ride every quarter creating an entirely new, people-powered network with a dedicated customer service, a state of the art mobile platform, and a fast-growing community of users, ShareCab is making travel social, money-saving and more efficient for millions of members.

Our customers


THE SHARECAB ECO-SYSTEM THRIVES ON THE DEMANDS OF OUR CUSTOMERS.

And our mission is to serve them well repeatedly, while learning from every request and every ride of every customer. Our ever-evolving eco-system has allowed our customers, both individuals and organizations, to simplify their transport needs. From our customers, we’ve learnt how much easier it is for them to forecast their transport spend, making it easier to budget and, ultimately, easier on their wallets. Large organizations, meanwhile, have built a close relationship with PickMe in order to help transport their teams, big and small.
